FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before you reach out.

How fast can you get out for an estimate?

We respond within one business day and can typically do a site visit within 3–5 business days. For urgent timelines, call us directly.

Do you work with general contractors or just homeowners?

Both. About 60% of our work is for GCs and commercial developers. The other 40% is residential homeowners.

Are you licensed and insured in Oregon?

Yes. We're Oregon CCB licensed and carry full general liability and workers' comp insurance.

What's included in a typical bid?

Our bids are itemized and flat-rate. We include equipment, labor, mobilization, and disposal. No surprise line items.

What areas do you serve?

Bend, Redmond, Sisters, Prineville, Sunriver, La Pine, Madras, Terrebonne, and surrounding Central Oregon communities.
